The Intake Process

How we lock in clients (Monthly Cycle).

  • 1. The "Open House": Monthly intake meeting. Invite potential clients to pitch/vibe.
  • 2. Founder Strategy Session: Private intake meeting with Core Team to review candidates.
  • 3. The Vote: Unanimous decision required. If we all agree, they're in.
  • 4. Lock-In: Deposit paid, timeline set, execution begins.
